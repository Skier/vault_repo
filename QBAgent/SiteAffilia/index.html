Home

Affilia is a custom application development company located in Dallas, Texas and Kiev, Ukraine. It was founded in 1999 by software engineers with extensive experience in full life cycle software development. We specialize in matching appropriate technical solutions to business problems through the use of object oriented methodology, a well-defined development approach, and Microsoft .NET and Java EE platforms.

At Affilia, we examine your IT infrastructure both in terms of your immediate needs and your long term business strategy. Our approach is customized to you, our client. It begins with an assessment of your current information technology system and a thorough understanding of your business needs and goals. We then help you tailor a comprehensive and cost-effective solution that is specific to your needs.

While we excel at capabilities which are typical to custom application development companies such as custom business application development, website design and development, backend systems development and integration, Affilia makes available unique capabilities such as combined onshore/offshore development and mobile solutions development. Affilia has a proven track record of successfully completing challenging software development projects on time and on budget. We excel at developing reliable, extensible software for mission-critical applications and essential business processes.

During our seven years in business we have completed multiple software projects and have excellent client references.
